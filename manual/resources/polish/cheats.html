For nds-bootstrap running from the system SD card, TWiLight Menu++ will have a cheats menu where you can toggle cheats between being enabled or disabled. The codes are in the action replay format, and they're packed into a usrcheats.dat file, which could be made using R4CEE. We recommend using the cheat database from DeadSkullzJr: https://bit.ly/DSJCheats

Place the cheat file in the /_nds/TWiLightMenu/extras folder on your SD card, then on compatible Nintendo DS titles, enter the per-game settings menu.
